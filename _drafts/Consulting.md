Managerial Consulting

It goes by many names:
Business consulting
Change-management

Whatever name you wish to call it, it is the general process of assisting organizations improve their performance.

When you hear the word "consultant," Management consulting is usually the first field of expertise that comes to mind.

Management consulting is a very broad field. In addition to change-management services, a management consultant may provide other services such as:

-Coaching
-Process analysis,
-Technology implementation, 
-Strategy development,
-or Operational improvement services,

These fancy words can all boil down to one thing: performance optimisation, 

and what better way to target the main performance drivers within a company: management.
