Alright, get it out of your system. Ask your questions:
Isn't 4Chan a path to the alt-right?
Aren't you an incel/ mysoginist if you visit 4Chan?
4Chan is part of the *gasp* darkweb?
No, no, and no.

I started visiting 4Chan when I was a freshman in highschool. I thought I was so bad. I felt no other kid could come close to my evil ways each time I typed the address "4chan.org" in the search bar.

I started out by visiting /b/. I heard rumors that it was the worst place on the world wide web to visit. And for a 14 year old kid, it was the worst. In a few minutes of scrolling, I was enlightened on how awful the world was. But at the same time I was facisinated. I felt as if I was interacting with the primordial ooze of the internet.

I also know now that /b/ isn't the worst place on the internet (there is way worse). 

In 14 years time, I've grown to appreciate the finer threads such as:
/biz/ Business & Finance
/fit/ Fitness
/g/ Technology
/lit/ Literature
/o/ Auto
/out/ Outdoors

But the one reason I continue to visit it 13 years later is because of these magical words:

Uncensored commentary.

If you want to interact with the primordial ooze of the internet, one of the best places to go is 4Chan. This has been the spawning ground of memes and technological trends (and yes, edgy philisophical ideals) since the early 2000s.
