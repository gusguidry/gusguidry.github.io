### Perfect is the enemy of the good.

I hate perfection.

I hate how I've deleted my site 3 or 4 times because I wasn't satisfied with any of them. They weren’t “perfect”.

The 'delete' key makes me loathe writing. I desire always to 'delete' but never do I keep things up on the internet for long. I'm terrified someone will pull some imperfect project I did from years ago and embarrass me.

I want to exercise confidence and leave content up for a little longer. Let’s see how things go.

Carrying on...

I'm on another Lord of the Rings craze. This is my second time reading all the books and watching the movies (director’s cut of course).

Right now I'm finishing up the Fellowship of the Ring. A couple of weeks ago I finished watching and reading The Hobbit. While the book was wonderful as always, I had to keep the vomit in during the unfortunate movie trilogy (except for the fight with the Nazgûl and Sauron. That was heavy metal).

Right now I'm on the part where the gang reached the mines of Moria in both in the book and in the movie.

Pretty great.
